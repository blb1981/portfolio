About me

I'm Brandon, a full-stack web developer with a specialty in JavaScript. I've been creating and running websites for myself and others for the last 15 years.

My most recent projects have focused around polishing my design skills, as well as becoming more familiar with React and Node.

Below you will find a sampling of my projects. Some are from years ago, and others more recent. I'd be glad to discuss the opportunity of working with you!
